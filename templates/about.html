About SmartShopper

What is SmartShopper?

SmartShopper is a fashion-focused price comparison tool that helps you find the best deals from Amazon, Flipkart, and Meesho — all in one place. We bring you real-time listings from top e-commerce sites and also include curated collections from Meesho to ensure you never miss out on budget-friendly, trending styles.

How it Works

When you search for an item, SmartShopper scrapes real-time listings from Amazon and Flipkart, while simultaneously pulling in relevant Meesho products from a live Google Sheet database. Our system detects keywords in your search to determine the most relevant categories and shows combined results.

Featured Categories

On the homepage, you’ll find quick-access categories like Tops, Dresses, Shoes, and Accessories. These are intelligently mapped to relevant data sources across all platforms and updated regularly.

Smart Filters

Filter your results by Lowest Price, Best Quality (based on ratings), Delivery Fee, or Review Count. It helps you make informed shopping decisions instantly.

Why Use SmartShopper?

No more switching between apps or tabs. Get a unified view of similar products across platforms and make smarter, faster purchases—all in one minimal, user-friendly interface.
